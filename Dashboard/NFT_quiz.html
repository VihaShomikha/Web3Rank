NFT

NFT, or Non-Fungible Token, is a unique digital asset that represents ownership or proof of authenticity of a specific item, piece of content, or collectible in the digital realm. Unlike cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin or Ethereum, NFTs are indivisible and cannot be exchanged on a one-to-one basis because each NFT has distinct properties and value. NFTs have gained prominence in various fields, including art, music, gaming, and collectibles, where they enable the ownership and transfer of digital or digitized real-world assets with verifiable scarcity and ownership on blockchain platforms.
